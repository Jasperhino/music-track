An audio fingerprint is a condensed digital summary of an audio signal, generated by extracting relevant features of audio content. In this project we attempt to visulize the sonic fingerprint of the top 100 songs of each decade starting at 1920. The data from the tracks sonic fingerprint is generated with Spotify’s streaming service algorithms for the recommendation of songs.
